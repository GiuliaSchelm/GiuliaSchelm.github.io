    About BLN #1 - Green places taken spaces

    The first issue of the city guide, About BLN #1, deals with green places and taken spaces in Berlin. The book focuses on abandoned areas that over decades have been taken over by residents. It describes locations such as the closed down airport, Tempelhofer Feld, the former US monitoring station on the Teufelsberg, or the abandoned theme park, Spree Park. The magazine is aimed towards all curious Berliners and tourists who want to discover the city anew. The Berlin U-Bahn network is an important reference that can be used to navigate through the city. The four tours that are suggested in the book always start at the nearest U-Bahn station and end right at the site.

    In collaboration with Antonia Aravena Llanca and Saskia Friedrich.
