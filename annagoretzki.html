    Anna Marie Goretzki

    Anna Goretzki is a Berlin based Journalist and Ethnologist. Her Articles are featured in the Süddeutsche Zeitung, Frankfurter Rundschau, on Deutschlandfunk, DRadio Wissen, Funkhaus Europa und DW Fernsehen (Latinoamérica). The website shows a selection of her work.
