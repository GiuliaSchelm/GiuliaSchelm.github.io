    Three Fairy Tales from the Brothers Grimm - After Hours

    Inspired by steganography - the art of concealing the existence of information within an seemingly innocuos carrier, this is a book of fairy tales that you don't quite expect. The encrypted meaning of this book will only reveal itself if you learn to read between the lines. Hidden in the back cover is a code booklet with which one can decipher the real story of the book, a pornographic short story. Solving page for page requires a fit mind and a big appetite for riddles.
