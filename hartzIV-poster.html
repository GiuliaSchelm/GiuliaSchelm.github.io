    "After your studies you can immediately apply for Hartz IV" - Poster

    While interviewing the 25 graduates of the graphic design class that were featured in the book After your studies you can immediately apply for Hartz IV we asked each individual to comment on this rather provacative and disencouraging statement of the same title. As a starting point we wrote down the statement on a big piece of transparent paper and asked each interviewee to comment on it as well as on the statements of earlier interviewees. This way we created an exchange of experiences between all participants. The orange lines connect the interviewees and form an abstract picture of the progression of the interviews. This picture also appeares on the cover of the book. The orange layer was silk-screen printed onto the Poster.
