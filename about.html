about

Giulia Schelm is a Berlin based graphic designer. After finishing her BA at the University of the Arts Berlin she cofounded the neukölln based design studio Burrow . Together with her 2 partners Lukas Küng and Philipp Koller their international and local client list includes cultural institutions, businesses, publishing houses and individiuals.
For recent work samples/references visit → www.im-burrow.de.
