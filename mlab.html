    Mlab

    Created the concept, realization and production of the yearly publication for the MLAB course. This year's Method Laboratory dealt with two main units: typography and grids. The book design consists of a leperello-like binding that is composed of two halves with each one presenting one unit. The book has to be flipped half way through to get to the second unit, therefore, the book has two covers. The information on the cover and the text on the accordion-like folded cardboard are silk-screen printed.
