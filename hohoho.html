    Ho Ho Ho

    This book shows the photographic work of 13 students of the University of Arts Berlin. In the seminar of Cologne-based photographer Thekla Ehling we were assigned to do different photo series throughout the winter semester of 2012. The final serie was suppose to capture christmas. For the publication we selected the strongest pictures of each student and put them together under the titel Ho Ho Ho.

    Layout and concept in collaboration with Idan Sher, Nora Heinisch and Isabel Kronberger
